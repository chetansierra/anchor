# Free Trial

Every new Nimbus workspace starts with a **14-day free trial of the Growth plan**.
No credit card is required to start the trial.

During the trial you get full access to Growth features: Sprint planning, GitHub
and Jira integrations, custom roles, and the public API. There are no limits on
projects or tasks during the trial.

When the trial ends:

- If you've added a payment method and chosen a plan, billing begins automatically
  on the plan you selected.
- If you haven't added payment, your workspace switches to a read-only state. Your
  data is preserved for 60 days, during which you can subscribe to restore full
  access. After 60 days of inactivity the workspace is scheduled for deletion.

You can start your subscription at any point during the trial from
**Settings → Billing**. Starting early does not shorten the trial — you still get
the full 14 days, and billing begins only when the trial ends.

To extend a trial for evaluation purposes, contact support.
