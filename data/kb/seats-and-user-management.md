# Seats and User Management

A **seat** is an active member of your workspace. Your bill is the number of active
seats times your plan's per-seat price.

**Inviting members.** Go to **Settings → Members → Invite** and enter email
addresses. Invited people who accept become active members and consume a seat at
the next billing cycle. Pending invites don't cost anything until accepted.

**Adding seats.** New members are added immediately and prorated for the rest of
the current billing period, so you're only charged for the time they're active.

**Removing seats.** Deactivate a member from the Members page. They lose access
right away, and the seat is freed at the **end of the current billing period** —
you won't be charged for that seat on the next renewal. Deactivated members can be
reactivated later without losing their assigned tasks and history.

**Guests.** You can invite external collaborators as **guests** with access to
specific projects only. On Growth and Enterprise, the first 5 guests are free;
beyond that, guests count as seats.

To see who's using a seat and when they last signed in, use the **Members**
activity view. For roles and access levels, see *Roles and Permissions*.
