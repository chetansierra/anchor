# Uptime and SLA

Nimbus is designed for high availability.

**Status page.** Real-time and historical uptime is published at
`status.nimbus.example`. You can subscribe there to get email or Slack alerts about
incidents and scheduled maintenance.

**Service Level Agreement (Enterprise).** Enterprise customers receive a **99.9%
monthly uptime SLA**. If we fall short in a given month, you're eligible for
service credits on a sliding scale:

- 99.0%–99.9%: 10% of that month's fee
- 95.0%–99.0%: 25%
- below 95.0%: 50%

To claim a credit, contact support within 30 days of the affected month. Credits
are applied to a future invoice and are the exclusive remedy under the SLA.

Scheduled maintenance is announced at least 48 hours in advance on the status page
and is performed during low-traffic windows; planned maintenance does not count
against the SLA.

Starter and Growth plans do not include a contractual SLA but run on the same
highly available infrastructure. For the formal SLA document, contact sales.
