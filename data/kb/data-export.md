# Exporting Your Data

Nimbus lets you export your data at any time — you own it, and there's no lock-in.

**Export a single project.** Open the project, then **Project menu → Export**, and
choose **CSV** (tasks as rows) or **JSON** (full structure including comments and
custom fields).

**Export the whole workspace.** Owners and Admins can go to
**Settings → Workspace → Export data** to generate a complete archive of every
project, task, comment, and attachment. Large workspaces are processed in the
background; we email a secure download link when the archive is ready, usually
within a few minutes to an hour. The download link is valid for 72 hours.

**What's included.** Projects, tasks, subtasks, comments, custom fields, labels,
and attachments. Member email addresses are included for owners. Audit logs are
exported separately on Enterprise.

**Programmatic export.** You can also pull data continuously through the public
API — see *API and Webhooks*.

Exports are available on every plan, including during a free trial and after
cancellation while your workspace is in read-only mode, so you can always take your
data with you.
