# Refund Policy

This policy explains when Nimbus issues refunds.

**14-day money-back guarantee.** If you're on a paid plan for the first time, you
can request a full refund within 14 days of your first charge, for any reason.
We'll refund the charge and move your workspace to the free read-only state.

**Monthly plans.** Outside the 14-day window, monthly charges are non-refundable.
When you cancel, you keep access through the end of the period you've paid for, but
we don't refund partial months.

**Annual plans.** If you cancel an annual plan, you can request a **prorated
refund** for the full, unused months remaining, minus any discount you received for
paying annually. The current month is not refunded.

**Duplicate or accidental charges** are always refunded in full — contact support
with the invoice number.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method and typically appear within
5–10 business days. To request a refund, go to **Settings → Billing → Invoices**,
open the charge, and click **Request refund**, or contact support. Enterprise
agreements follow the refund terms in the signed contract.
