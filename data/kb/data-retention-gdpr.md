# Data Retention and GDPR

Nimbus is built to help you meet your data-protection obligations, including the
GDPR and CCPA.

**Where data lives.** By default, workspace data is hosted in the United States.
Enterprise customers can choose **EU data residency** (Frankfurt) at workspace
creation.

**Retention.** Active workspace data is kept as long as your subscription is
active. After cancellation, data is retained for 90 days, then permanently
deleted. Free trials that aren't converted are deleted after 60 days of
inactivity. Backups are kept for 30 days on a rolling basis.

**Right to access and erasure.** Workspace owners can export all data (see
*Exporting Your Data*) and can permanently delete the workspace and its data from
**Settings → Workspace → Delete workspace**. To delete a single member's personal
data, deactivate and then remove the member; their personal data is purged within
30 days while project history is anonymized.

**Sub-processors and DPA.** A current list of sub-processors and a signable Data
Processing Addendum are available on request from support or your account manager.
Nimbus does not sell personal data or use customer content to train models.
