# Pricing and Plans

Nimbus offers three plans, billed per active seat per month. Annual billing saves
roughly 20% versus monthly.

- **Starter — $10/seat/month.** Up to 10 members, unlimited projects, Kanban and
  Calendar views, Slack integration, and 30-day activity history.
- **Growth — $18/seat/month.** Everything in Starter plus Sprint planning,
  GitHub and Jira integrations, custom roles, the public API, and 1-year history.
- **Enterprise — custom pricing.** Everything in Growth plus SAML single sign-on,
  audit logs, a 99.9% uptime SLA, data residency options, and a dedicated success
  manager.

All paid plans include email support; Growth and Enterprise add priority support.
You can change plans at any time from **Settings → Billing → Plan**. Upgrades take
effect immediately and are prorated; downgrades take effect at the end of the
current billing period.

Seat counts are based on active members. Deactivating a member frees their seat at
the next billing cycle. For questions about volume discounts or nonprofit pricing,
contact our sales team to book a callback.
