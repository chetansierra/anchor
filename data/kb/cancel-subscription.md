# Canceling Your Subscription

You can cancel your Nimbus subscription at any time — there are no cancellation
fees and no minimum contract on the Starter and Growth plans.

**How to cancel:**

1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Plan**.
2. Click **Cancel subscription**.
3. Confirm. You'll see the date your paid access ends.

**What happens after you cancel:** Your plan stays active until the end of the
current billing period — you keep all paid features until then, and you are not
charged again. When the period ends, the workspace switches to a read-only state:
you and your team can sign in and view or export everything, but can't create or
edit. Your data is retained for **90 days** after cancellation so you can
resubscribe and pick up where you left off.

To permanently delete your workspace and data instead of leaving it read-only, use
**Settings → Workspace → Delete workspace**.

Cancelling stops future charges but does not by itself issue a refund for the
current period — see the *Refund Policy* for when refunds apply. Enterprise
contracts follow the cancellation terms in your agreement.
