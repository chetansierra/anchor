# Getting Started with Nimbus

Welcome to Nimbus, the team workflow and project management platform. This guide
walks a new admin through first setup.

1. **Create your workspace.** After signing up, you'll be prompted to name your
   workspace. The workspace is the top-level container for all your projects,
   members, and billing.
2. **Invite your team.** Go to **Settings → Members → Invite** and add teammates
   by email. Invited users receive a link to join your workspace. See
   *Seats and User Management* for how invites affect billing.
3. **Create your first project.** Click **New Project**, pick a template (Kanban,
   Sprint, or Calendar), and add a few tasks to get a feel for the board.
4. **Connect an integration.** Most teams connect Slack first so task updates
   post to a channel. See *Integrations* for the full list.

A typical workspace is up and running in under ten minutes. If you're migrating
from another tool, read *Importing Data* before inviting your whole team, so your
historical projects come across cleanly.

Need help during onboarding? Our support team can walk you through setup — see
*Contacting Support* to book a call.
