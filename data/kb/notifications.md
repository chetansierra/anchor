# Notifications and Email Preferences

Nimbus notifies you about the work that matters to you, across email, in-app, and
push.

**Notification types.** You can be notified when you're assigned a task, mentioned
in a comment, when a task you follow changes, when a due date approaches, and for
daily or weekly digests.

**Managing your preferences.** Go to **Settings → Notifications** to turn each type
on or off per channel (email, in-app, mobile push). Choices are per person, so
every member controls their own notifications.

**Digests.** Instead of real-time emails, you can switch to a once-a-day or
once-a-week digest that bundles updates into a single email. Great for reducing
inbox noise.

**Snooze and mute.** Mute a specific project to stop notifications from it, or
snooze all notifications during focus time and outside working hours. Set your
working hours under **Settings → Notifications → Schedule**.

**Not getting emails?** Check spam, confirm the email type is enabled, and ask your
admin to confirm `notifications@nimbus.example` isn't blocked by your mail server.
For push specifically, see *Mobile App*.
