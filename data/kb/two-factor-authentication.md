# Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

Two-factor authentication adds a second step to sign-in, requiring a one-time code
in addition to your password.

**Enabling 2FA:**

1. Go to **Settings → Account → Security → Two-factor authentication**.
2. Choose **Authenticator app** (recommended) or **SMS**.
3. For an authenticator app, scan the QR code with Google Authenticator, Authy, or
   1Password, then enter the 6-digit code to confirm.
4. Save your **backup codes** somewhere safe. Each code works once and lets you
   sign in if you lose your device.

**Workspace-wide enforcement:** Admins on the Growth and Enterprise plans can
require 2FA for all members under **Settings → Security → Require 2FA**. Members
who haven't set it up will be prompted on their next sign-in.

**Lost your device?** Use a backup code to sign in, then re-enroll a new device.
If you've lost both your device and your backup codes, an admin can reset 2FA for
your account from the Members page. If you're the only admin, contact support to
verify your identity and regain access.
