# Importing Data

Moving to Nimbus from another tool? You can bring your projects and tasks with you.

**Supported imports:**

- **CSV** — map columns (title, description, assignee, due date, status, labels) to
  Nimbus fields. Best for spreadsheets and tools that export CSV.
- **Trello** — import boards, lists, and cards directly by connecting your Trello
  account; lists become columns and cards become tasks.
- **Asana** and **Jira** — guided importers that preserve projects, tasks,
  assignees, and comments where possible.

**How to import:** Go to **Settings → Import**, choose a source, and follow the
wizard. Imports run in the background; you'll get an email and an in-app summary
when they finish, including any rows that were skipped and why.

**Tips for a clean import:**

- Make sure assignee emails in your file match the emails of members you've already
  invited, so tasks are assigned correctly.
- Import into a fresh project first to review the result before importing at scale.
- Large imports (50k+ tasks) are throttled to protect performance; contact support
  to schedule very large migrations.

Imported data can be exported again at any time — see *Exporting Your Data*.
