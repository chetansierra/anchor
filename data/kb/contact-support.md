# Contacting Support

We're here to help. Here's how to reach a human and what to expect.

**Email support** (all plans). Write to `support@nimbus.example` or use the in-app
**Help → Contact us** button. Include your workspace name and, for billing issues,
the invoice number.

**Response times:**

- Starter: within 1 business day.
- Growth: priority queue, typically within 4 business hours.
- Enterprise: 1-hour response for urgent issues, 24/7, plus a dedicated success
  manager.

**Book a callback.** Prefer to talk it through? You can request a callback and
we'll schedule a time that works for you. Share your name, email, and a one-line
summary of what you need, and the team will reach out — this is also the fastest
way to talk to **sales** about Enterprise, SAML SSO, volume pricing, or migrating
a large team.

**Report a bug or outage.** Check `status.nimbus.example` first. If the issue
isn't listed, contact support with steps to reproduce and screenshots.

**Security issues.** Email `security@nimbus.example` for responsible disclosure.

When you reach out, our assistant may capture your details and create a ticket or
book a callback so the right person follows up.
