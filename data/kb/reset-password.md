# Resetting Your Password

If you've forgotten your password or want to change it, you can reset it yourself
in a couple of minutes.

**If you're locked out (forgot your password):**

1. Go to the Nimbus sign-in page and click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter the email address on your account and submit.
3. We'll email you a password reset link. The link is valid for **60 minutes**.
4. Open the link, choose a new password, and you'll be signed in automatically.

If the reset email doesn't arrive within a few minutes, check your spam folder and
confirm you used the correct email. Reset links are single-use; request a new one
if it expires.

**If you're already signed in and want to change your password:**

Go to **Settings → Account → Security → Change password**, enter your current
password, then your new one.

Passwords must be at least 10 characters and include a number. If your workspace
uses SAML single sign-on, password reset is handled by your identity provider, not
by Nimbus — see *Single Sign-On*. Accounts with two-factor authentication will be
prompted for their 2FA code after resetting.
