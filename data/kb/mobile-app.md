# Mobile App

Nimbus has native apps for **iOS and Android**, available on the App Store and
Google Play. Sign in with the same credentials you use on the web, including SAML
SSO.

**What you can do on mobile:**

- View and update tasks, change status, and comment.
- Get push notifications for mentions, assignments, and due dates.
- Create tasks quickly, including from a shared link or photo.
- Switch between multiple workspaces.

**What's web-only (for now):** workspace and billing settings, building automation
rules, and the admin Members/Roles screens. Manage those from a browser.

**Offline support.** Recently viewed projects are cached so you can read them
offline; changes you make sync automatically when you reconnect.

**Requirements.** iOS 16+ or Android 9+. The app updates itself through the app
stores; enable automatic updates to stay on the latest version.

If push notifications aren't arriving, check that notifications are enabled both in
the Nimbus app settings and in your device's system settings — see *Notifications
and Email Preferences*.
