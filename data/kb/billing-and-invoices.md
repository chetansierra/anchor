# Billing and Invoices

Nimbus billing is managed from **Settings → Billing**, available to workspace
owners and members with the Billing Admin role.

**Payment methods.** We accept major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard,
American Express) through our payment processor. Enterprise customers can pay by
ACH or wire transfer against an invoice with NET-30 terms.

**Billing cycle.** Subscriptions renew automatically on the same calendar day each
month (or each year for annual plans). Seat changes during a cycle are prorated.

**Invoices and receipts.** Every charge generates an invoice you can download as a
PDF from **Settings → Billing → Invoices**. To add a VAT/GST number, company name,
or billing address that should appear on invoices, edit **Billing details** before
your next renewal.

**Failed payments.** If a charge fails, we retry over several days and email the
billing contact. The workspace keeps working during the retry window; if all
retries fail, it moves to read-only until payment succeeds.

For questions about a specific charge, contact support with the invoice number.
Refund eligibility is covered separately in our *Refund Policy*.
