# Website AI support agent (RAG over your docs)

**Service id:** rag_support_agent · **Typical price:** $300–800 (fixed scope)

A chatbot that reads your existing help docs, FAQ, and product pages and answers customer questions instantly — **with citations to the source** — so visitors get the right answer without waiting on email or search. This is the most common starting point and the demo on this site (Nimbus) is exactly this service.

## When this fits

- You have documentation, a help center, FAQ, or knowledge base and customers keep asking the same questions.
- You want to cut support load and answer questions on the website 24/7.
- You care that the bot answers *from your content* and doesn't make things up.

## What's included

- Ingestion of your docs/site (load → chunk → embed → index). Re-indexing is one call when your docs change.
- A grounded RAG agent that answers with inline citations to the source document.
- Anti-hallucination guardrails: it refuses or escalates when your docs don't cover the question, instead of guessing.
- An embeddable widget — one `<script>` tag, shadow-DOM isolated so it never clashes with your site's styles, themeable to your brand color.
- Hosting guidance, rate-limiting, and a hard daily cost ceiling so a bot or bored visitor can't run up your API bill.

## Add-ons

- Lead capture / booking on top (see the lead-capture agent).
- An evaluation report proving accuracy on your real questions.
