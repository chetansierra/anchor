# About Chetan and how these AI agents are different

I'm Chetan, a backend engineer with ~4.5 years building production systems (Java, Kafka, Go) in the finance domain. I build **production-grade AI agents on top of your real data** — agents that are evaluated, observable, and don't break in production.

## The positioning

The market is flooded with "I build AI agents" and no-code automations. My edge is coding the hard 20% that Make/n8n and prompt-templates can't: custom tool-calling and webhooks into your systems, real hosting, anti-hallucination guardrails, an evaluation harness that proves accuracy with numbers, and cost/latency observability so you know what it costs to run.

So this is **not** generic "AI consulting." It's productized, evaluated engineering: a working agent, a number that says how accurate it is, and a dashboard that shows every run's cost.

## What I build (the productized services)

- **Website AI support agent** (`rag_support_agent`) — a RAG chatbot over your docs/site that answers with citations.
- **AI lead-capture / booking agent** (`lead_capture_agent`) — an agent that answers *and* captures the lead or books the call into your CRM.
- **"Automate this workflow" with AI** (`workflow_automation`) — connect an AI API to your tools to automate a repetitive workflow (the coded version of an n8n gig).
- **Agentic function** (`agentic_function`) — a multi-step agent that acts as a sales assistant, email manager, or similar role.
- **AI internal-tooling integration** (`internal_tooling`) — wire AI into your internal apps and dashboards.
- **Agent reliability audit** (`reliability_audit`) — run my eval harness on your existing bot.
