# Agentic function (sales assistant, email manager, and similar roles)

**Service id:** agentic_function · **Typical price:** $500–1,200 (scoped to the role)

A multi-step agent that takes on a standing *role* in your business rather than a single one-shot task. It plans, calls multiple tools, and works toward a goal across a short sequence of steps — for example an AI sales assistant that qualifies inbound leads and books meetings, or an email manager that triages your inbox, drafts replies, and flags what needs you.

## When this fits

- You want an AI that *acts* like a team member for a narrow role, not just answers questions.
- The job needs several steps and tools: read, decide, take an action, check the result, continue.
- Examples: AI sales manager / SDR assistant, email/inbox manager, research-and-summarize agent, onboarding assistant.

## What's included

- An agent loop with planning and multi-tool orchestration (the model decides which tool to call and when).
- Integrations with the systems the role touches (CRM, email, calendar, internal APIs).
- Guardrails, an iteration cap so it can't loop forever, and human approval gates on costly actions.
- Evaluation on representative scenarios plus full observability (every step, tool call, and cost is traced).

## Honest scoping

I'll tell you plainly which parts are reliable to automate now and which should stay human-in-the-loop. The goal is a dependable, bounded agent that adds real value — not an over-promised "does everything" bot.
