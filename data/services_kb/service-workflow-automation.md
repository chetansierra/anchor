# "Automate this workflow" with AI

**Service id:** workflow_automation · **Typical price:** $200–600 (fixed scope)

You have a repetitive, rules-plus-judgment task that eats time every day — triaging incoming emails, tagging and routing tickets, summarizing documents, extracting fields from messy text, drafting replies. I connect an AI model to your tools and automate it. Think of it as the coded, reliable version of an n8n or Zapier flow, without the brittleness when the input doesn't fit the template.

## When this fits

- A person currently copies data between tools, classifies things, or writes the same kind of message over and over.
- You tried a no-code automation and it broke on the messy 20% of cases.
- The task needs judgment (summarize, classify, extract, decide) that simple rules can't handle.

## What's included

- A scoped automation: one workflow, clearly defined inputs and outputs.
- AI calls wired to your tools (email, sheets, ticketing, internal APIs) with retries and error handling.
- Guardrails and a human-in-the-loop checkpoint where a wrong action would be costly.
- Logging so you can see what it did and what it cost.

## Examples

- Auto-summarize and route incoming support emails to the right queue.
- Extract structured fields from PDFs/invoices into a sheet.
- Draft first-pass replies for a human to approve.

For multi-step, role-like automation (a standing "sales assistant" or "email manager"), see the agentic function service.
