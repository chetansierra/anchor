What the agent actually does

Three jobs, done reliably.

Not a generic chatbot bolted onto a prompt — a retrieval-grounded agent that only speaks from your content, and acts when it should.

1

Reads your docs

Your help center, FAQs and policies are ingested and indexed. The agent searches them on every question — so it answers from your content, not the open internet.

2

Answers — or refuses

It replies with the exact sources it used, and when your docs don't cover something it says so and offers a human instead of making something up.

3

Takes the action

When a visitor wants pricing or a call, it captures the lead or books the callback straight into your CRM — turning a question into a conversation you can close.
